SpiderVine

Body-powered line travel & movement gear for the commons.

MythGear README

What it is

SpiderVine is a human-powered system for moving along a suspended line or cable using hands, core, and legs rather than motors or passive glide.

The body does the work. The line carries the path.

What it’s for

  • Full-body movement and conditioning
  • Skill-building through attention and feedback
  • Utility: hauling, cleanup, temporary access, rescue support
  • Serious play with real consequences

What it’s not

  • Not a zipline
  • Not motorized
  • Not optimized for speed or ease

SpiderVine prioritizes control and repairability over convenience.

Status

Early notes are fragmented, but the concept is anchored here to prevent drift.

Design is rebuilding carefully, with safety and learning held together.

SpiderVine — part of the MythGear commons. Movement through gravity, without motors.
