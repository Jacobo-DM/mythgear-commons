MythGear Commons

Elumannín + Jacobo
Open hardware · Open storywork

An open hardware / open storywork lab for movement medicine: gloves, shoes, field-kits, and resonance tools that help people listen with their whole bodies – in the garden, on the trail, and in the city.

Purpose

  • Design gear that supports somatic literacy – learning through movement, breath, and touch.
  • Keep everything as commons-friendly as possible: repairable, remixable, and shareable.
  • Bridge MythWear, RageDance, CodeTape, and other Flower Road projects.
movement medicine open hardware commons

Design Principles

  • Jute & Grit – favor natural fibers, tactile feedback, and honest materials.
  • Multi-use – every element should do more than one thing when possible.
  • Local repair – designs should be fixable with simple tools and community skill.
  • Open patterns – share patterns and blueprints under Creative Commons licenses.
