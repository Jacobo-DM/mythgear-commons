SunHearth Rhyme & Safety Mantra

For Circle of Warmth lessons, class openings, and gentle reminders.

SunHearth Rhyme

SunHearth, SunHearth, tiny and bright, Turn a little flame into gentle light. Warm our hands and warm our air, Teach us how to tend with care. SunHearth, SunHearth, quiet and small, A circle gathers — you warm us all.

You can clap softly on each line, or tap once on the table every time you hear the word SunHearth.

Safety Mantra

We watch the flame. We let it stay. We never touch — that’s the safest way. We keep things clear. We keep things bright. We learn from warmth, but treat fire right.

This can be said together before any demonstration that uses a small supervised flame. Students should know that they never light, move, or blow out the SunHearth on their own.

The SunHearth is for supervised classroom inquiry only. These words are here to help us remember that curiosity and safety walk together.
